McGill Association of North American Born Asians, more commonly known as MANABA, is a non-profit, student-run organization based at McGill University in Montreal, Canada. Beyond representing the fusion of Asian and North-American culture, MANABA celebrates people of all ethnicities and backgrounds. Through events, MANABA hopes to bring the young and lively Montreal community together.
